There's a lot going on in Seattle in the summer. Here is a list of fun events, attractions and restaurants to keep you busy during our wedding weekend!

Events

Friday, July 31

  • Seafair Fleet Week & Boeing Maritime Celebration: Ship Tours, 9:30 - 3:30, Seattle Waterfront (FREE)
  • Out To Lunch Summer Concert: The Staxx Brothers, 12 - 1:30, South Lake Union Park (FREE)
  • Seattle Chamber Music Society Summer Festival, 7 PM Recital, 8 PM Concert, Benaroya Hall
  • Galacticon 4: the largest Battlestar Galactica celebration in the world, July 31 - Aug 2, Seattle Center

Saturday, August 1

  • Seafair Fleet Week & Boeing Maritime Celebration: Ship Tours, 9:30 - 3:30, Seattle Waterfront (FREE)
  • Seattle Sounders vs Vancouver Whitecaps (soccer), 7 PM, CenturyLink Field
  • Mohai (Museum of History & Industry) Movies in the Park: The Great Gatsby, 6 PM, Lake Union Park (FREE)
  • Seattle Center Movies at the Mural: Selma, 9 PM (dusk), Mural Amphitheatre at Seattle Center (FREE)

Sunday, August 2

  • Boeing Airshow (Blue Angels!) and Hydroplanes Race, 8:30 AM - 6 PM, Genesee Park/Lake Washington (FREE)
  • Seafair Fleet Week & Boeing Maritime Celebration: Ship Tours, 12:30 - 3:30, Seattle Waterfront (FREE)
  • Waterfront Whimsea, 11 AM - 3 PM, The Hot Spot at Waterfront Park (FREE)
  • Free Blues and Cool Jazz in Freeway Park, 2 - 4 PM, Freeway Park (FREE)
